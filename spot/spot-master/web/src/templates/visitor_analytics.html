Meet Our Team

Zachary Johnston

Chief Executive Officer

Zachary ("Zack") is a third year electrical engineering student at UC San Diego. Zack grew up in two places—California and Japan—and his worldview is impacted by this cross-cultural upbringing. His love for coffee started when he was three years old, and he's been addicted to caffeine ever since. Zack first sketched the initial design of this product in 2016 after being frustrated by how long it took to wait for iced coffee. He wanted it cold and he wanted it fast. And Polar Coffee was born... at least on a scrap piece of paper.

 

Jesus Fausto

Chief Financial Officer

Jesus, the cofounder of Polar Coffee, studies electrical engineering at UC San Diego. His passion for entrepreneurial engineering and his technological vision equipped him to bring Polar Coffee to life. Jesus has assisted Dr. Jack Silberman in the design of a smart wheelchair.

 

Jesi Miranda

Chief Technology Officer

Jesi is a third year electrical engineering student at UC San Diego. Despite his specialty in power systems, Jesi is a gifted programmer, fluent in LabView, C, Python, and a myraid of other langauges. Under the direction of Dr. Jack Silberman, Jesi helps lead the construction of a smart wheelchair.

 

John Ho

Director of Software Development

John Ho is a third year electrical engineering student at UC San Diego. His past software work revolves around 3D virtual reality character animation, in addition to device to device communications.
